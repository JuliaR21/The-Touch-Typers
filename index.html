Menu

Our menu highlights the very freshest fish that Chef Rose is able to source from around the world. She takes great care in his technique and ingredients; expertly butchering the fish, perfecting the flawless sushi rice and offering hand-shaved, fresh wasabi.

The menu features Kozara (Japanese small plates), such as Ahi Tuna Tacos, Truffle. Chawanmushi and Edamame "Guac" with Rice Crisps. For the sushi purist, Chef Rose offers a full selection of Classic Sashimi and Nigiri selections, Temaki (hand rolls) and Hosomaki (traditional Japanese rolls).

For the guests desiring something other than sushi, the menu also features Ohzara (Japanese main plates), such as Caramel Lacquered Short Rib and Roasted Salmon. To place an order or make a reservation, call +44 7738680002.
