About Us

Sushi and the City is a revolutionary service offered to hard working Londoners looking for luxury yet affordable food choices during the day. Bought to you by 2 x Michelin Star Chef Julia Rose, Harvard educated food tech entrepreneur Aalia Shah, and restauranteur Lydia Finnegan, who currently owns three top restaurants in Mayfair, these ladies bring you fine dining straight to the desk. Located in the heart of London, we offer an array of sushi, sashimi and teppanyaki. Prepared fresh each day, with delicious local ingredients and top quality standards in our kitchen. Sushi And The City provides quick, easy, nutritious yet luxury food at an affordable price. We pride ourselves on bringing you the best service in the City.
